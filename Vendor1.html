Four essential facts

1. You decide your level of investment. Set a daily budget that meets your needs. We generally recommend ensuring you have enough budget to get impressions, clicks, and sales.
2. You can change your budget at any time. Increase or decrease your daily budget in seconds—even when your campaigns are running—to meet your evolving needs.
3. You can see the impact on your sales. A sale is attributed to a campaign when a shopper that clicks an ad purchases your brands products on our website within 14 days. Our reporting lets you chart your ad-attributed sales.
4. You can control your message. Our solutions let your reinforce your brands value proposition, helping you tell an authentic and engaging story to our shoppers.
